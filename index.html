Advisory de pares, no de consultores Peer advisory, not consultants

Zoo es la red de advisory ejecutivo de CBA Board. Nuestros asesores no son consultores externos: son CEOs y líderes senior que han operado empresas reales, tomado decisiones difíciles y generado resultados medibles en mercados como el tuyo. Zoo is CBA Board's executive advisory network. Our advisors aren't external consultants — they are CEOs and senior leaders who have operated real companies, made hard calls, and delivered measurable results in markets like yours.
